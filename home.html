What's Inside

Everything your club needs,
right in their pocket.

From the final whistle score to supporter photos after the game — Local Rugby Hub keeps your community connected all season long.

📊

Live League Table

Real-time standings with points, wins, draws, losses, points scored and conceded. Updated the moment a score is entered.

📅

Fixtures & Results

Full season schedule at a glance. Tap any match for a detailed breakdown including tries, bonus points and venue.

👥

Squad Profiles

Starting XV, bench and player profiles with position, jersey number and photo. First, second and third XV all supported.

📰

Club News Feed

Match reports, announcements and general posts from your club admin. Fans can comment and engage with every post.

📸

Supporters Gallery

A dedicated photo gallery for game day moments, supporter pics and behind-the-scenes shots — shared by your admin.

🏆

Man of the Match

Fans vote for their Man of the Match after every game. Results are visible in the match detail screen.

🔮

Score Predictions

Fans predict match scores before kick-off. See how your prediction compares to everyone else's after the final whistle.

📈

Stats & Analytics

Win/loss ratios, form guides (last 5 results), attack vs defence rankings, biggest wins and tournament highs — all computed automatically.

⭐

Player of the Month

Club admins can pin a Player of the Month to the club profile — visible to all followers on the club's home screen.

Getting Started

Up and running in minutes.

For fans, there is nothing to set up. Download, follow your club and you're done.

1

Download the App

Free on Google Play. No subscription, no hidden fees — ever. Open the app and follow your favourite club or competition.

2

Follow Your Club

Search for your club or competition, tap Follow and your home screen fills up with fixtures, results and club news instantly.

3

Sign In to Engage

Create a free account with Google or email to vote for MOTM, predict scores and leave comments on posts.

4

Clubs: Go Admin

Club representatives can apply to become admins. Once approved, you control your squad, scores, posts and gallery.

For Club Administrators

Put your club
on the map.

Give your supporters the experience they deserve. As a club admin you get full control over your club's presence on the app.

  • ✅
    Enter match scores, tries and bonus points in real time
  • 👤
    Manage your full squad — starters, bench, player profiles and photos
  • 📣
    Publish match reports, announcements and general news posts
  • 📸
    Upload photos to the Supporters Gallery after every game
  • ⭐
    Crown a Player of the Month that appears on your club profile
  • 🆓
    Completely free — no cost to your club, ever
