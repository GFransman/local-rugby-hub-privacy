Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Local Rugby Hub account and all associated personal data at any time.

What gets deleted

  • Your Firebase account (name, email, profile photo)
  • Your display name
  • Any app data linked to your account

What is not affected

  • Followed clubs and competitions saved locally on your device (you can clear these by uninstalling the app)

How to request deletion

Send an email to us from the email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 7 days.
