How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to request the deletion of your Swartland Rugby Connect account and associated data, you can do so in one of the following ways:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account within the app.

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to garth.fransman@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered email address.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • Any personal data associated with your account

Account deletion requests will be processed within 7 days.
